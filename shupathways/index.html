About SHU Pathways

Pathways is an exciting networking event that takes place in March every year.

Individuals and businesses come together to discuss current trends and employment opportunities in broadcast journalism, film and media production, animation, visual effects, gaming, digital media, public relations, and photography.

You can get a taste of last year's event by watching the video below or exploring the site further. If you have a question, don't hesitate to contact us either with the form at the bottom of this page or by email - info@shupathways.co.uk

SHUCase

We invite you to discover an array of stunning creative pieces created by our very own students or even submit your own work to be displayed for others to admire!

You can browse through the exceptional work produced last year, featuring students from various creative courses such as Animation, Graphic Design, Game Development, Media and even Journalism.

FAQ

Got a question? Head below to see if we can answer it. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us - info@shupathways.co.uk

How can I contact you?

Send us your emails to info@shupathways.co.uk. Or contact us on our many social media pages.

How do we find our room on the day?

Our room numbers are located in the schedule section. However, the numbering system maybe confusing for some people. If the room is 9201 for example, the 9 means the the building (which is always the cantor), the 2 would be the floor you are on. The floor for this instance would be the 2nd floor. The final two numbers 01 suggest the room number on that floor. There will be directions on the day and feel free to ask a member of our team.

How do I book an event?

Just go to the schedule section, pick your chosen event and click either 'book now' or 'guest booking' (book now is for Sheffield Hallam students and staff only). Then, pick your event and follow the instructions on screen.

Where is Pathways 2019 located?

Pathways is located in the Cantor building at Sheffield Hallam. If you are wondering where the building is based, please have a look at our map and address here.
