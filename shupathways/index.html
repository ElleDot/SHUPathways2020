    With Roger Eccleston, Deputy Vice Chancellor at SHU
    (Duration: 15mins)

    Roger Eccleston

    Deputy Vice Chancellor at Hallam, Roger Eccleston will kick Pathways 2020 off with a welcoming opening address and speak of the spotlight Pathways places on young people and local businesses, along with all the things to come throughout the day!

  • 11:00am | Creative Lounge

    With Dawn Walton and Richard Knight from Screen Yorkshire
    (Duration: 45mins)

    Dawn Walton & Richard Knight

    Dawn and Richard are both professionals with lots of experience in the film-making industry. In this talk, they'll explain personal situations and deliver exciting information on what it's like in their day-to-day lives and what it means to be a film-maker in 2020!

  • 11:00am | Showroom 5

    With Luxmy Gopal, Abigail Jaiyeola & Altine Bootha-King from Look North and the Barnsley Chronicle (Duration: 45mins)

    Making Headlines in Regional News

    In this talk, experienced and talented individuals Luxmy Gopal, Abigail Jaiyeola and Altine Bootha-King with detail the current situation in the world of Journalism across platforms, be it on the web or aired on national TV.

  • 12:00pm | Creative Lounge

    With Suchandrika Chakrabarti, creator of the Freelance Pod, Will Roberts from Baldlygo & Sam Taylor from Enki Music
    (Duration: 45mins)

    Making it as a Freelancer

    Suchandrika is a journalist, speaker and freelancer who has years of experience in her fields.
    She is joined by Will Roberts, who runs his own project called 'Baldlygo', and Sam Taylor, a music consultant from Enki Music.
    The three of them have all worked (or currently are working) as a freelancer, and in this talk, you will learn what it takes to work and how to find your clients.

  • 12:00pm | Site Gallery

    With Nikki Stearman, Katy Davies, Ellie Cunningham and Jess Gaskell from Dubit Ltd. and Sumo Digital
    (Duration: 45mins)

    Making Games - Dubit Ltd. & Sumo Digital

    Dubit Ltd. - "With nearly twenty years under our belt, working with startups and global kids brands, Dubit are the largest kids agency in the world."

    Sumo Digital - A global games company with offices in Sheffield and dozens of well known titles in their repertoire, like the Forza Horizon and Little Big Planet series.

  • 1:00pm | Creative Lounge

    With Jess Brammar, Editor-in Chief at Huffington Post
    (Duration: 45mins)

    Diversity in the Media - Jess Brammar

    With Rax Lakhani - PRCA,
    Shannon Cooke - Light Cinemas
    & Melissa Lawrence - Taylor Bennett Foundation
    (Duration: 45mins)

    Getting Noticed - PR and Digital Marketing Strategies

In this talk, you will learn about common practices for leading a great marketing scheme, and why PR is your secret best friend!

About SHU Pathways

Pathways is an exciting networking event that takes place in March every year.

Individuals and businesses come together to discuss current trends and employment opportunities in broadcast journalism, film and media production, animation, visual effects, gaming, digital media, public relations, and photography.

You can get a taste of last year's event by watching the video below or exploring the site further. If you have a question, don't hesitate to contact us either with the form at the bottom of this page or by email - info@shupathways.co.uk
